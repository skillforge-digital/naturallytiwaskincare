The Ritual

Three calm steps. No drama, no product overload.

The best version of a sensitive-skin routine is one that feels protective, not punishing. Start simple, keep friction low, and let the texture do some of the work.

  1. Warm the formula.

    Rub a small amount between your palms so the balm softens before it meets the skin.

  2. Press, then smooth.

    Work gently over dry, reactive areas instead of rubbing aggressively into them.

  3. Repeat consistently.

    Use after washing and before bed so the barrier gets the same support every day.
